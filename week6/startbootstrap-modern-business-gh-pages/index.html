Thy Location

Mount Everest, the highest mountain on Earth, has an elevation of 8,848.86 meters (29,031.7 feet) above sea level. It is located in the Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the Himalayas, straddling the border between Nepal (Province No. 1, Solukhumbu District) and the Tibet Autonomous Region of China. Known locally as Sagarmatha in Nepali and Chomolungma in Tibetan, Everest is a major destination for mountaineers and adventurers worldwide.
